<p>Hallo! My name is Krikamol Muandet (ไกรกมล หมื่นเดช). I am a <a href="https://is.mpg.de/en/careers-at-mpi" target="_blank">research group leader</a> affiliated with the <a href="http://ei.is.tuebingen.mpg.de/" target="_blank">Empirical Inference Department</a> at <a href="http://www.is.mpg.de/en" target="_blank">Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems</a>, Tübingen, Germany. From January 2016 to December 2017, I was a lecturer at the <a href="http://www.sc.mahidol.ac.th/scma/" target="_blank">Department of Mathematics</a>, <a href="http://science.mahidol.ac.th/" target="_blank">Faculty of Science</a>, <a href="http://www.mahidol.ac.th/en/" target="_blank">Mahidol University</a> in Thailand. My research interest lies in the area known as "<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_learning" target="_blank">machine learning</a>". I am particularly interested in, for example, statistical learning theory, kernel methods, Bayesian nonparametric, large-scale learning, and counterfactual prediction. When I am not doing research, I enjoy reading books (on topics related to philosophy, psychology, history of science, etc) and watching movies. I also like outdoor sports such as bouldering, climbing, and snowboarding.</p>
