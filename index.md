<h2>Welcome</h2>
<p>Hallo! My name is Krikamol Muandet (ไกรกมล หมื่นเดช). I am currently a chief scientist and tenure-track faculty (fast track) at <a href="https://cispa.de/en" target="_blank">CISPA</a> - Helmholtz Center for Information Security which is within the <a href="https://www.helmholtz.de/en/" target="_blank">Helmholtz Association</a>. From 2018 to 2022, I was a <a href="https://is.mpg.de/en/careers-at-mpi" target="_blank">research group leader</a> affiliated with the <a href="http://ei.is.tuebingen.mpg.de/" target="_blank">Empirical Inference Department</a> at <a href="http://www.is.mpg.de/en" target="_blank">Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems</a>, Tübingen, Germany. From January 2016 to December 2017, I was a lecturer at the <a href="http://www.sc.mahidol.ac.th/scma/" target="_blank">Department of Mathematics</a>, <a href="http://science.mahidol.ac.th/" target="_blank">Faculty of Science</a>, <a href="http://www.mahidol.ac.th/en/" target="_blank">Mahidol University</a> in Thailand. I graduated <i>summa cum laude</i> with a PhD degree specializing in kernel methods in machine learning. My PhD advisor was <a href="https://www.is.mpg.de/~bs" target="_blank">Prof. Bernhard Schölkopf</a>. I also obtained a master’s degree with distinction in machine learning from <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk" target="_blank">University College London (UCL)</a>, United Kingdom. At UCL, I worked primarily in the <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/gatsby/" target="_blank">Gatsby Unit</a> with <a href="https://www.stats.ox.ac.uk/~teh/" target="_blank">Prof. Yee Whye Teh</a>.</p>

<p>My research interest lies in the area known as "<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_learning" target="_blank">machine learning</a>".
I am particularly interested in, for example, statistical learning theory, kernel methods, causal inference, counterfactual prediction, and algorithmic game theory. When I am not doing research, I enjoy reading books (on topics related to philosophy, psychology, history of science, etc) and watching movies. I also like outdoor sports such as bouldering, climbing, and snowboarding.</p>

<p>I am indebted to all my <a href="{% link student.md %}">students</a> and <a href="{% link collaborator.md %}">collaborators</a> for our exciting collaborations. I always seek new collaboration, so I will be glad to hear from you.</p>
